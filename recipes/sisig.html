Sisig

Description

"repudiandae sunt praesentium. Vitae quidem sunt ipsa pariatur eaque,

rerum illum deserunt atque molestias dolor similique

dignissimos laudantium repudiandae?"

Ingredients

  • 1/4 lb. chicken liver chopped
  • 1 1/2 lbs. grilled chicken breast finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 cup minced yellow onion
  • 1 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 pieces serrano pepper sliced diagonally
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1 egg
  • and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • two 5 tablespoons mayonnaise

Steps

  1. add 1 1/2 lbs. grilled chicken breast finely chopped
  2. then 1/4 lb. chicken liver chopped
  3. 1 cup minced yellow onion ready
  4. 1 teaspoon garlic powder in the pot
  5. 1 pieces serrano pepper sliced diagonally
  6. 1 tablespoons soy sauce poured in the pot
  7. 1/4 cup butter as well
  8. most importantly two 5 tablespoons mayonnaise
  9. and 1 egg
  10. and pepper to taste
  11. add 2 tablespoons cooking oil in the pot
